CESPA

Christ Episcopal School Parents' Association



CESPA's mission is to strengthen the entire school community by:
  1. Develop a positive and welcoming climate for all parents by providing open communications, avenues for parents to interact and become active;
  2. Work closely with the administration and faculty and assist them in achieving their goals and meeting current needs
  3. Support the philosophy, purposes and objectives of the school through its activities
