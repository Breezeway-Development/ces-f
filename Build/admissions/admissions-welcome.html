Welcome to Christ Episcopal School



Christ Episcopal School is situated on two sites. Students in Prekindergarten and Kindergarten are on a campus which is adjacent to the Bogue Falaya Park in historical, downtown Covington. Students in first through twelfth grades are located on a spacious, wooded campus off of Highway 21 near Interstate 12 in Covington. Take our campus tour online, come to our Prospective Parent Open House for in early October of each year or call to make an appointment to schedule a tour. Our high school open house is held mid November of each year for grades eighth through twelfth. If you would like more information or would like an admissions packet, complete the inquiry form or call Pam Perry at 985-871-9902 to request a packet.

The school's program is based on a broad, liberal arts curriculum enhanced with strong enrichment in art, music, Spanish, technology, physical education and wilderness education. Christian education is also an integral part of the program. Students attend weekly chapel service and scheduled Eucharist celebrations throughout the year. Extracurricular activities include drama, choir, and ceramics in addition to participation in basketball, volleyball, flag football, track and cross-country.

We look forward to hearing from you and having the opportunity to explore the possibility of having your family become a part of the Christ Episcopal School community.
